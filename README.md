An end-to-end Power BI customer analytics solution that explores rider behavior, membership trends, ride patterns, and station intelligence using Cyclistic Bike Share data.

## Introduction

This project was designed to transform raw bike-sharing data into actionable customer intelligence by analyzing rider behavior, usage patterns, station performance, trip characteristics, and membership trends. By leveraging Power BI's advanced analytics and visualization capabilities, this solution enables data-driven decision-making to support Cyclistic's business objectives.

The primary business objective is to understand the behavioral differences between annual members and casual riders, enabling Cyclistic to identify opportunities for increasing membership conversions and optimizing customer retention strategies.

## Skills Showcased

- ⚙️ **Data Transformation (ETL) with Power Query:** Cleaned, consolidated, and standardized multi-source bike-sharing datasets by handling missing values, validating ride records, enforcing data consistency, and preparing data for analytical modeling.

- 📱 **DAX Measures & KPI Development:** Developed calculated measures and business KPIs to evaluate ridership performance, customer behavior, ride duration trends, station utilization, and membership distribution metrics.

- 📈 **Customer Behavior Analytics:** Analyzed differences between annual members and casual riders through customer segmentation, ride frequency analysis, temporal usage patterns, and behavioral comparison metrics.

- 📊📉 **Core Visualizations:** Utilized **Line Charts, Bar Charts, Column Charts, Scatter Plots, Donut Charts, Matrices, Maps, and KPI Cards** to identify trends, compare rider segments, and uncover actionable insights.

- 🗺️ **Geospacial Analysis:** Leveraged **Map Visualizations** to analyze ride origins, station popularity, geographic demand distribution, and customer mobility patterns across the Cyclistic network.

- 📈 **KPI Dashboards & Business Reporting:** Created executive-level **KPI summaries** to monitor total rides, average ride duration, membership distribution, station performance, and customer engagement metrics.

- 🎨 **Dashboard Design & Storytelling:** Designed a business-focused and intuitive dashboard experience that transforms raw ride data into actionable customer intelligence through effective visual communication.

- 🖱️ **Interactive Reporting & User Experience:**
  - **Slicers:** Enabled dynamic filtering by customer type, bike type, season, weekday, station, and ride characteristics.

  - **Drill-Through Analysis:** Allowed users to move from high-level performance metrics to detailed customer, station, and ride-level insights for deeper investigation.

- 🎨📎📐 **Business Intelligence & Customer Analytics:** Applied analytical techniques to uncover rider behavior patterns, identify membership conversion opportunities, evaluate usage trends, and support strategic business decisions.

## Data Preparation & Analytics Process

The project includes a complete analytics workflow consisting of:
- Multi-file data ingestion and consolidation
- Data auditing and quality assessment
- Missing value identification and handling
- Duplicate record removal
- Ride duration validation and outlier filtering
- Feature engineering for time-based analysis
- Customer segmentation analysis
- Data type standardization for Power BI integration
- Data quality reporting and documentation
- Business-focused KPI generation and visualization

## Key Business Questions Addressed
- How do annual members and casual riders differ in their riding behavior?
- When are bikes used most frequently throughout the day and week?
- Which stations experience the highest rider demand?
- What ride duration patterns characterize each customer segment?
- Which bike types are most popular among different rider groups?
- What seasonal and temporal trends influence customer usage?
- Which customer behaviors indicate potential membership conversion opportunities?

## Conclusion

This dashboard demonstrates how Power BI can transform raw bike-sharing data into a comprehensive customer analytics and decision-support platform. Through data cleaning, feature engineering, KPI development, and strategic visualization, the solution provides actionable insights into customer behavior and operational performance.

The project provided valuable insights into how analytical techniques can be used to understand customer preferences, identify usage trends, optimize operational decisions, and support strategic initiatives aimed at increasing membership conversions and maximizing customer lifetime value.
